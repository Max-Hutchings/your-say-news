How others voted
— display options

Seven ways to render the same aggregated, anonymised breakdown. The goal: lead with totals and distribution, not just percentages, so it's obvious how many people in each group voted and which way they leaned. Same data in every option — pick the ones worth building.

Recommendation

Ship A (Split columns) as the default and offer B (Diverging) and E (Table) as switchable views via a small control in the sheet header. A covers the "distribution + sentiment" story you're after; B shines on polarised posts; E is the escape hatch for exact numbers. C and D are strong alternates worth keeping in the picker.
